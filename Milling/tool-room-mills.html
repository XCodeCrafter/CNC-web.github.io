Tool Room Mills

Tool Room Mills

Milltronics has a long history of building tool room machines that can be run as manual, teach or full CNC with thousands of satisfied users. Popular in tool rooms, job shops, and tool & die, these machines are very flexible and can be used for a wide variety of different parts.
We offer two types of quill machines in a traditional knee style (VK) as well as a bed type (TRQ). The rigid head (TRM) machines are available in four different sizes starting with a compact 30 x 16 in model up to a large travel 78 x 33 in.

The TRM3016 is a tool room mill that offers large travels, speed, and power at a great price. The TRM3016 (pictured below) has “drop down” door openings for long part pass-through to help accommodate oversize parts.

Tool Room Mills

VK4II

The VK4II is a full 3-Axis CNC knee mill with the unique Milltronics MillSlideTM that provides greater rigidity and more spindle nose to table top than conventional knee mills. With 33″ x 14.25″ x 5.25″ (MillSlideTM), 16.5″ knee and 5.83″ quill travel, the VK4II can be run as manual, teach or full CNC.
